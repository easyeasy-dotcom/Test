How to Use Your AI Tracker

Every EasyEasy filter set comes with an NFC chipset for smart tracking. Follow these simple steps to unlock the full potential of your AI-powered journey.

Step 1: Find Your NFC Chip

When you receive your EasyEasy filter set, you'll find a small NFC chip included in the no.1 package. It looks like a small card or sticker.

Step 2: Tap with Your Phone

Simply hold your smartphone near the NFC chip. It works with:

  • iPhone (iPhone 7 and newer with iOS 14+)
  • Android phones with NFC enabled

No app download required! Your phone will automatically detect the chip.

Step 3: Access Your Personal Dashboard

After tapping, you'll be automatically redirected to your personal EasyEasy AI Tools tracking page. This is your control center for monitoring your smoking reduction journey.
